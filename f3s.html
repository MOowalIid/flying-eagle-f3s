OVERVIEW:

Flying Eagle Skates are known to challenge many high-end models for fractions of the price, and the Flying Eagle F3S Origami Skates are no different.

The rugged shell boot is supportive, with ventilation holes keeping the foot cool and providing extra flexion. The boot construction will benefit users who want decent energy transfer with fewer pressure points on foot, reducing fatigue!

A durometer of 85A provides a smooth a grippy roll. Freestyle skating enthusiasts may benefit from upgrading this part.

The liner is exceptional for the price. Soft padding keeps the foot comfortable. If you are using your skates for long periods, a liner like this is very much appreciated.
